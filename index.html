The Woodhouse Group is a Texas based governmental affairs firm offering results-oriented legislative and regulatory strategies. The Woodhouse group provides clients with access to a group of consultants with extensive experience inside the Texas Senate and Texas House of Representatives, and behind the scenes of political campaigns.
